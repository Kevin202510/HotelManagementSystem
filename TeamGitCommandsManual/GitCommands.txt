***** CLONING OUR PROJECT  *****

1. git clone then copy the url from the github.

***** END OF CLONING *****


***** PUSHING YOUR WORK TO THE PROJECT *****

1.git status
2.git add .
3.git commit -m "What You Do Or What You Are Pushing to the project"  .. note(para malaman namin kung ano ba ginawa nyo or may ginagawa ba kayo..)
4.git push

***** END OF PUSHING *****

***** PULLING OUR PROJECT IF WE DID SOMETHING OR MAY GINALAW SA PROJECT *****

1.git status
2.git add .
3.git commit -m "What You Do Or What You Are Pushing to the project"
4.git push
5.git pull

***** END OF PULLING PROJECT THAT EXIST TO YOUR SYSTEM UNIT *****

***** PULLING WITHOUT DOING ANYTHING *****

1.git pull

***** END OF PULLING *****
